Metabolites

Metabolites arte intermediate products of metabolic reactions catalysed by various enzymes that naturally occure within cells. The term metabolites used for small molecules.

  • The API and Drug product in the body are exposed to various enzymes from which metabolite impurities can be formed.
  • Drug metabolism is traditionally divided into two phases: metabolic (i.e. heaptic) clearence and Phase I and Phase II process.
  • The division is based on the observation that drug substance first undergoes oxidative attack (e.g. benzene to phenol) and the newly introduced hydroxyl function undergo glucouronidation (e.g. phenol to phenyl glucouronic acid).

Some metabolite are formed as impurities during the developement of a process.
