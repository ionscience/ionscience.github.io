Chemical Space Docking

In recent years computation derug design and development made huge impact and hence virtual compound screening using virtual library. The current screening library is larger and more diverse, which can be applied to identify more effective hit compounds and open up a new chemical space represented by a new core scaffold.


Chemical space docking is a novel approach for virtual screening which allows for handling multiple molecules occurring in combinatorial chemical spaces. Chemical space docking aims to predict actives based on their binding mode after a docking procedure with subsequent scoring of the complexes. Yet the source of the docked molecules is no longer an enumerated library but a combinatorial built-up 'space' of zillions of molecules.
